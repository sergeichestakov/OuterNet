Constituency NA-120 (Lahore-III) ( Urdu : این اے-۰۲۱، لاهور-۳ ‎) is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan .

Election 2002 [ edit ]

Further information: Pakistani general election, 2002

By-election 2017 [ edit ]

Further information: Panama Papers case
Further information: NA-120 by-election, 2017

Following the decision given by the Supreme Court of Pakistan to disqualify Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif from public office, a by-election was triggered in his NA-120 constituency. The by-election took place on 17 September 2017. Kulsoom Nawaz won this seat with 61,745 votes. [1]

All candidates receiving over 1,000 votes are listed here.
