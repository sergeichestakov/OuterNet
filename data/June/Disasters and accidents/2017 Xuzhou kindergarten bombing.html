2017 Xuzhou kindergarten bombing

On 15 June 2017, a bombing at a kindergarten in Feng County , Xuzhou , Jiangsu province, east China , killed at least eight people and injured 65 others. [1] [2] [3] The perpetrator, 22-year-old Xu Taoran, died in the blast.

Explosion [ edit ]

According to the district government, the blast, which occurred at 4:50, did not kill any kindergarten students or teachers. The blast happened at the entrance on June 15 while children were leaving the Chuangxin Kindergarten in Fengxian. Two died on the spot and five succumbed to injuries at the hospital. Nine are still critical.

The explosion was the result of a suicide bombing, in which the perpetrator, a 22-year-old man with surname Xu, also died. The bomber had dropped out of school but was employed, and had health problems. [4]

By late night, eight people had died, sixty-five were injured, with eight in a critical condition. The public security department were treating it as a criminal case. [5] [6] [7] [8]
