Presidential election [ edit ]

Main article: Indian presidential election, 2017

A presidential election was held in India on 17 July 2017 before the term of the President Pranab Mukherjee ended. Ramnath Kovind was elected as the President of India .

Vice Presidential election [ edit ]

Main article: Indian vice-presidential election, 2017

A Vice Presidential election were held in India on 5 August 2017 before the term of Vice President Mohammad Hamid Ansari 's term ended. Venkaiah Naidu was elected as the Vice President of India.

Legislative assembly elections [ edit ]

Punjab [ edit ]

Main article: Punjab Legislative Assembly election, 2017
Punjab

Elections for the State assembly were held on February 04, 2017 with 75% voter turnout. [3] [4] In Punjab all the 117 assembly constituencies have triangular contests between major parties makes the results uncertain. [5]

Result was declared on 11 March 2017.
