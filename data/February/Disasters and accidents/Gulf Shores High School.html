Gulf Shores High School is a high school in Gulf Shores, Alabama , United States that serves grades 9 - 12 and is part of the Baldwin County Public Schools in Alabama . The school is at the center of a plan to build a combination high school-junior college campus which would alleviate overcrowding at the present school while providing a close link with Faulkner State Community College . [1]

Administration [ edit ]

Gulf School is part of the Baldwin County Public Schools system and is one of nine high schools in the county. The Superintendent of Education for the county is Eddie Tyler.

School principal, Cindy Veazey was a principal at Wetumpka high.
