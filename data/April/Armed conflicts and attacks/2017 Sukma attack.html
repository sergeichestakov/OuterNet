The Sukma Attack was an ambush carried out by the Communist Party of India (Maoist) (CPI) against Indian paramilitary forces on 24 April 2017, during the Naxalite-Maoist insurgency , which ended up being the largest since a similar 2010 attack in a neighbouring district . [1] It took place between Burkapal and Chintagufa in Sukma district of Chhattisgarh , India . [2] [3] [4] [5]
