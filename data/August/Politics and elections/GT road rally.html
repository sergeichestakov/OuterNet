GT road rally
Date August 9–12, 2017 ( 2017-08-09 – 2017-08-12 )
Duration 4 days
Location mainly Punjab, Pakistan
Also known as Homegoing rally
Type Political rally
Cause Panama Papers case
Organised by Pakistan Muslim League (N)
Participants Public and PML (N) workers
Deaths 1 child [1]

GT road rally (also known as Homegoing rally) was a Pakistani political rally organized by Pakistan Muslim League (N) for the former Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif . [2] [3] [4] [5] [6] [7] [8] [9] [10]

On 11 August 2017, a child was killed in Lalamusa by cars in rally's motorcade . [1]
