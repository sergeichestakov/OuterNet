List of Danish consorts

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation , search

This list of Danish consorts includes each queen consort (wife of a reigning king) and each prince consort (husband of a reigning queen). Due to unions ( personal and real ), the queens of 1380–1814 were also queens of Norway , and the queens of 1389–1520 were also (though with interruptions) queens of Sweden .
