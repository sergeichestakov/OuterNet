Statistics [ edit ]

In 2008 there were 162,527 crimes reported in Minnesota, including 109 murders.In 2015 one of the Highs Crime problem was the aggravated assault crime with 7,094 crimes reported in Minnesota [1]

Capital punishment laws [ edit ]

Capital punishment is not applied in this state. [2]
