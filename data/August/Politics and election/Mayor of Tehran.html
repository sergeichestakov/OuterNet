The Mayor of Tehran is an elected politician who, along with the Tehranâs City Council of 31 members, is accountable for the strategic government of Tehran. Since 27 August 2017, Mohammad-Ali Najafi is the mayor of Tehran. Previously, the position was held by Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf , who held office for 12 years.
