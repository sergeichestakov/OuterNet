This list of traffic collisions records serious road crashes : those that have a large death toll , occurred in unusual circumstances, or have some other historical significance, which are notable and have their own Wikipedia article.

2000s [ edit ]

2000 [ edit ]

  • August 28 – Nigeria – Abuja bus crash riots , Abuja, Nigeria. Seventy people are killed in a bus pile up in Abuja. The next day, related protest riots kill four more people.
  • November 5 – Nigeria – Ibadan road tanker explosion , Ibadan, Nigeria. Between 100 and 200 people are killed when a petrol tanker loses control, slams into a traffic jam and explodes.

2001 [ edit ]

  • February 28 – United Kingdom – Great Heck rail crash , Selby, England. Car driven off the M62 motorway near a bridge onto railway tracks, causing the derailment of a passenger train and its subsequent collision with a freight train.
  • March 5 – Portugal – Hintze Ribeiro disaster , Castelo de Paiva , Portugal . A bridge collapsed as a bus was crossing the Douro in Portugal, killing 59 people. [1]

2002 [ edit ]

  • May 26 – United States – I-40 bridge disaster , Webbers Falls, Oklahoma . 14 people died and 11 others were injured on Interstate 40, when a barge collided with a bridge support, causing a 580-foot section of the bridge to plunge into the Arkansas River.

2003 [ edit ]

  • 1 May – South Africa – Bethlehem bus crash , Bethlehem, South Africa . 80 people drown when the driver drove off a dam and into a reservoir. [2]
  • 4 June – India – Kasganj level crossing disaster , Kasganj , Uttar Pradesh . 30 people die when a bus attempted to cross a closed level crossing in India and was sliced in half by a train.
  • 23 November – Malaysia – Merapoh bus tragedy , near the Pahang - Kelantan border. 14 passengers were killed in a bus tragedy at kilometer 63 of Lipis and Merapoh roads (Federal Route 8) near Merapoh, Pahang.

2004 [ edit ]

  • 19 March – Finland – Konginkangas bus disaster , Äänekoski , Finland. 23 people killed.
  • 6 November – England – Ufton Nervet rail crash , Ufton Nervet , Berkshire . Seven are killed and 71 injured when a passenger train hits a car and derails.

2005 [ edit ]

  • 3 February – India – Nagpur level crossing disaster , Nagpur , Maharashtra . 55 wedding guests die when a crowded trailer being towed by a tractor was hit by a train.
  • 27 April – Sri Lanka – Polgahawela level crossing accident , Polgahawela . Over 50 people were killed when a bus is hit by train.
  • 7 June – Nepal – Badarmude bus explosion , Badarmude. 38 people were killed when a bus struck a land mine.

2006 [ edit ]

  • 18 February – Australia – Cardross road accident , Cardross, Victoria . 6 people were killed in an alleged hit and run accident.
  • 17 April – Mexico – Maltrata bus crash , Maltrata. 57 people die in a bus crash.
  • 23 June – Egypt – Sinai bus crash , between Nuweiba and Taba . 11 Arab Israeli tourists are killed when their bus overturns.
  • 30 September – Canada – De la Concorde overpass collapse , Laval , Quebec . Five are killed and six injured when a 65-feet section of a 3-lanes overpass over Autoroute 19 collapses. [3]
  • 13 November – South Africa – Faure level crossing accident , Faure near Somerset West . A truck carrying workers is hit by a train. 19 are killed and 6 injured.
  • 20 November – United States – 2006 Huntsville bus crash , Huntsville, Alabama . 4 high school students were killed and 23 injured when a school bus went over the side of an elevated portion of Interstate 565. [4]

2007 [ edit ]

  • 6 January – Bangladesh – Comilla bus crash , Comilla. A bus travelling from Dhaka to Chittagong crashes and catches fire, killing from 55 to 70 people. [5]
  • 2 March – United States – Bluffton University bus crash , Atlanta, Georgia . 7 people are killed when a bus transporting the Bluffton University baseball team crashes through a barrier and lands on Interstate 75.
  • November 4 – Azerbaijan – Baku–Gazakh motorway minibus crash , Qaradağ Raion , Baku , Azerbaijan . A minibus, carrying twenty persons, slammed into a parked truck laden with stones. 14 died and four were injured. [6]

2008 [ edit ]

  • April 19 - Spain - Benalmádena coach crash , Benalmádena, Spain. 38 injured and 9 killed after an SUV collided with a bus full of tourists while trying to overtake it. [7]
  • December 14 – Egypt – 2008 Egyptian bus accident , Dahrut, Egypt. A bus plunged into an irrigation ditch while traveling from Cairo to Minya killing at least fifty-five and injuring ten.
  • December 16 – Israel - 2008 Israeli tour bus crash , southern Israel. 25 killed and 30 injured after a bus crashes off the road and rolls down a ravine between Ovda and Eilat in Israel. It is the deadliest traffic accident in Israel's history. [8] [9]

2009 [ edit ]

  • July 24 – Russia – 2009 Rostov-on-Don bus crash , Rostov Oblast . At least 21 people were killed when a bus collided with a petrol tanker. [10]
  • July 26 – United States – 2009 Taconic State Parkway crash , Mount Pleasant, New York . Eight were killed, including the driver, and three were injured when a driver travels the wrong way for 2.7 kilometres (1.7 mi) on the Taconic State Parkway in New York State before crashing head-on into a sport utility vehicle. [11]
  • August 14 – Romania – Scânteia train accident , Scânteia , Iaşi County . At least 14 people died after a bus-train collision on County Road 248C. [12]

2010s [ edit ]

2010 [ edit ]

  • February 13 – Nigeria – Nigerian bus electrocutions , Port Harcourt , Rivers State . A power line broke, striking two buses and electrocuting the occupants. [13]
  • February 22 – Peru – 2010 Peru bus crash , Virú , Peru. Two buses collided head-on on the Panamerican Highway resulting in at least 38 deaths and the injury of 58. [14]
  • July 2 – Democratic Republic of Congo – Catastrophe of Sange , Sange , South Kivu . A fuel tanker overturned and exploded, creating a fireball that devastated the town of Sange in South Kivu province. The preliminary death toll placed the number of dead at 220, with 111 injured, though the numbers later increased. [15]
  • October 12 – Ukraine – Marhanets train accident , Marhanets , Dnipropetrovsk Oblast . A bus ran a red light and crossed a railroad crossing before getting hit by a train, resulting in 43 dead and 9 injured. [16]
  • December 20 – Malaysia – 2010 Cameron Highlands bus crash , Blue Valley in the Cameron Highlands . Twenty-seven people, mostly Thai tourists, were killed when the double-decker bus they were traveling in crashed at the Second East–West Highway of the Perak–Pahang state border. Until 2013, the worst traffic accident in Malaysian history. [17]

2011 [ edit ]

  • March 12 – United States – World Wide Tours bus crash , Bronx, New York . A bus crashed on Interstate 95 killing 15 people. [18]
  • November 4 – England – 2011 M5 motorway crash , near Taunton , Somerset. A 34-vehicle crash near Taunton, England on the M5 motorway resulted in 7 dead and 51 injured. [19] [20]

2012 [ edit ]

  • March 13 – Switzerland – Sierre coach crash , Sierre , Switzerland. A coach carrying school teachers and pupils crashed into a wall in the Sierre Tunnel. Of the 52 people on board, 28 were killed in the crash. [21]
  • April 20 – Mexico – Álamo bus accident , Alamo, Veracruz . A trailer truck collides with an overloaded passenger bus, 43 killed, 17 seriously injured. [22]
  • July 12 – Nigeria – Okobie road tanker explosion , Okobie . A road tanker overturned and exploded; the death toll was initially placed at 95. [23]
  • August 26 – China – Shaanxi bus–tanker crash , Yan'an , Shaanxi . A double-decker sleeper bus and a tanker loaded with highly-flammable methanol collided, resulting in 36 deaths. [24]

2013 [ edit ]

  • May 7 – Mexico – 2013 Ecatepec de Morelos gas tanker explosion , Ecatepec de Morelos , State of Mexico . A tank truck driver lost control and ran into several cars and houses before the truck exploded on Federal Highway 85. 27 people were killed and more than 30 injured. [25]
  • June 23 – Montenegro – 2013 Podgorica bus crash , near Morača Monastery , Kolašin Municipality . A bus carrying Romanian tourists plunged into a ravine, killing 19 and injuring 28 more.
  • July 13 – Russia – 2013 Oznobishino bus crash , Oznobishino , Moscow Oblast . A truck carrying gravel collided at high speed with a bus. At least 18 people died and another 60 people were injured.
  • July 28 – Italy – 2013 Monteforte Irpino bus crash , Monteforte Irpino , Italy . A tour bus returning from a pilgrimage went off the highway into a ravine, killing 39 and injuring 19 others. [26]
  • August 21 – Malaysia – 2013 Genting Highlands bus crash , Genting Highlands , Pahang . A bus carrying 53 passengers crashed into ravine killing 37 passengers were killed and injuring 16 others. Deadliest road accident in Malaysia. [27]
  • September 5 – South Africa – 2013 Pinetown crash , Pinetown . An articulated truck drove through a red light at high speed, killing 24 people and injuring at least 80. [28]
  • September 18 – Canada – Ottawa bus-train crash , Ottawa , Ontario . A double-decker bus and a passenger train collided, killing 6 and injuring 35. [29]
  • October 12 – Peru – 2013 Peru bus disaster , Santa Teresa District . A makeshift bus carrying 51 Quechua Indians plunged off a cliff and ended up in the Cachimayo River, killing everyone on board. The deadliest accident in Peruvian history. [30]
  • October 19 – Philippines – 2013 Atimonan road crash , Atimonan , Quezon . Three buses and four trucks collided, 20 killed and 54 injured. [31]
  • October 30 – India – 2013 Mahabubnagar bus accident , Mahabubnagar District , Andhra Pradesh . A bus hit a culvert, damaging its fuel tank and causing a fire, killing 45 people and injuring another 7. [32]
  • December 16 – Philippines – 2013 Manila Skyway bus accident , between Parañaque and Taguig . A bus fell off the Metro Manila Skyway , killing 18 and injuring 20. [33]

2014 [ edit ]

  • February 4 – Ukraine – 2014 Ukraine train bus collision , Sumy Oblast . A bus was hit by a train killing 13 and injuring 6. [34]
  • April 13 – Mexico – Acayucan bus crash , Juan Rodriguez Clara . A bus traveling from Villahermosa to Mexico City collided with a broken-down tractor-trailer attached to a truck and then caught fire. 36 were killed and another 4 injured. [35]
  • November 11 – Pakistan – 2014 Khairpur bus crash , Khairpur . A bus collided with a truck, killing 56. [36]
  • December 22 – Scotland – 2014 Glasgow bin lorry crash , Glasgow . A garbage truck drove up onto a sidewalk in George Square , killing 6 and injuring 15. [37]

2015 [ edit ]

  • March 11 – Tanzania – 2015 Tanzania road accident , Iringa Region . A bus and two trucks collided, resulting in 41 dead and 25 injured. [38]
  • October 23 – France – Puisseguin road crash , Puisseguin . A bus carrying elderly day-travelers hit a truck head-on and caught fire, killing 43. [39]
  • December 14 – Argentina – 2015 Argentina road accident , Rosario de la Frontera . A bus carrying Argentine frontier police plunged off a bridge in rural northern Argentina, killing 43 people on board and injuring 8 other people. [40]

2016 [ edit ]

  • February 18 – Ghana – Bus collision in February 2016, Ghana . Head-on collision between a bus and a truck, 71 killed and at least 13 injured. [41]
  • May 8 – Afghanistan – May 2016 Afghanistan road crash , Moqor district, Ghazni province . Two buses collided with a fuel tanker, killing 73 and injuring 50 more. [42]
  • June 7 – United States – In the Kalamazoo bicycle crash a pickup truck crashed into a group of cyclists killing five.
  • July 19 – Taiwan – 2016 Taoyuan bus fire , Taoyuan . A tour bus caught fire killing 24 Chinese tourists, a Taiwanese tour guide, and the driver. [43]
  • August 15 – Nepal – 2016 Nepal bus crashes , Birtadeurali , Kavrepalanchok District and Siddheshwar , Baitadi District . Two separate bus crashes in Nepal led to the deaths of 30 people, 52 injured. [44] [45]
  • September 3 – Afghanistan – September 2016 Afghanistan road crash , Zabul Province . A bus collided with a fuel tanker; 38 people were killed and 28 were injured. [46]
  • November 21 – United States – 2016 Chattanooga school bus crash , Chattanooga, Tennessee . An elementary school bus carrying dozens of students crashed, killing 6 students and injuring 23 others. [47]

2017 [ edit ]

  • January 21 – Italy – 2017 Verona bus crash , Verona . A bus carrying school children from Hungary crashed and caught fire on the A4 near Verona a few minutes after midnight. [48]
  • February 20 – Philippines – 2017 Tanay bus accident , Tanay, Rizal . A tourist bus carrying 50 passengers crashed on a curved road after the vehicle apparently lost control and hit an electric post in Barangay Sampaloc in Tanay, Rizal. [49]
  • April 18 – Philippines – 2017 Nueva Ecija bus accident , Carranglan, Nueva Ecija . A minibus carrying 77 passengers lost its brakes and fell off a ravine in Carranglan , Nueva Ecija . It is recorded as one of the deadliest road accidents in recent Philippine history, leaving 31 dead and 46 injured. [50]
  • August 26 - United Kingdom - 2017 M1 motorway crash , Buckinghamshire . Two lorries and a minibus collide. [51]
