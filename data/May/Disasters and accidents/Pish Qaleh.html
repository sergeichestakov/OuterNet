Pish Qaleh ( Persian : پيش قلعه ‎‎, also Romanized as Pīsh Qal‘eh ; also known as Qal‘eh ) [1] is a city in Maneh District , Maneh and Samalqan County , North Khorasan Province , Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 1,631, in 465 families. [2]
